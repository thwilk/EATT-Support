About EATT2

EATT2 is an accountability tool designed specifically for lacrosse players to track their extra work and commitments. It helps players stay organized, log progress, and remain accountable to their team and coaches. This app is not a performance trackerâ€”it focuses on off-field commitments and personal improvement.

Key Features

  • Player Profiles: Enter your position, graduation year, and group number.
  • Task Tracking: Log extra work and submit updates easily.
  • Accountability: Track your own progress over time.
  • Secure Cloud Sync: Your submissions are safely stored in the cloud.
  • Simple Interface: Designed for players, intuitive and clean layout.

Who Should Use EATT2

Our app is intended for lacrosse players who want to stay on top of their extra work and commitments. Coaches and teams can ensure their players remain accountable without tracking on-field performance data.

Privacy & Security

All player data is stored securely in the cloud. We do not collect unnecessary personal information, and your progress is only visible to you and your authorized coaches.
